Experience

June 2022 - August 2022

Summer Internship

I completed my mandatory first internship at Vakıf Global, focusing on mobile application development. During this internship, I developed a goal-tracking application using Flutter and SQLite. The application was intended for internal company use, aiming to make it easier for employees to view the tasks assigned to them. The application featured two distinct roles: Admin and Personnel. Admin Role: The admin could assign tasks to personnel, delete tasks, or update tasks, performing full CRUD operations. Additionally, the admin could track the completed and remaining tasks of personnel through graphical reports. Personnel Role: Personnel could view their assigned tasks and track them under two separate tabs: "Completed" and "Remaining." They could also see statistics of their completed tasks compared to their remaining tasks through graphical insights. This project was a valuable experience in mobile development and provided practical insights into task management systems.

May 2023 - June 2023

Summer Internship

In 2023, I joined İm Park Bilişim to complete my second mandatory internship. I dedicated my internship to learning artificial intelligence concepts. By the end of my internship, I developed the TPNG application. The purpose of the TPNG application is to detect the coordinates of questions in a question bank book and separate them into tests, writing the results into an XML file. Using YOLO, I enabled the detection of a total of 16 different classes, such as question boundaries. I utilized free OCR tools like PyTesseract and EasyOCR to ensure that the XML file was correctly organized into tests according to the proper question order. This project, which I developed during the final days of my internship, allowed me to continue working at İm Park Bilişim even after the internship ended.
